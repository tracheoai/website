About Daikokuten Enterprises

Daikokuten Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. is a next-generation innovation and R&D company focused on solving critical healthcare challenges through cutting-edge medical technology and interdisciplinary research.

Founded by Dr. Nikhlesh Tiwari, a distinguished neurosurgeon with over 15 years of experience, our mission is rooted in practical clinical insight. Driven by a passion to create intelligent, lifesaving tools where conventional methods fail, Dr. Tiwari leads a dynamic team of engineers, doctors, and researchers.
